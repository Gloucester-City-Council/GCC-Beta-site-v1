Vehicle licensing

Standards, equipment and inspection requirements for hackney carriage and private hire vehicles in Gloucester. Includes fees and approved testing station details.

Hackney carriage vehicle requirements

ℹ️
All hackney carriage vehicles must be white. All newly licensed hackney carriage vehicles must be wheelchair accessible.

Age and emissions

First licence
The vehicle must be no more than 7 years old and meet Euro 6 emissions standard.
Maximum age
A vehicle cannot be licensed after its 15th anniversary of first registration.
Wheelchair accessible vehicles (WAVs)
WAVs are exempt from the 7-year first licence age limit but must meet Euro 5 or above. The 15-year maximum still applies.

Testing and inspection

  • Annual test by a Gloucester City Council licensing authority approved test station
  • Six-monthly tests once a vehicle is 8 years old
  • An MOT is required unless the vehicle is under 1 year old

Plates and signage

  • A rear exterior plate and an interior plate are required; both must be visible
  • A standardised illuminated 'TAXI' roof sign must be displayed; illuminated only when the vehicle is available for hire
  • Council recognition panels on the doors must include the city crest and plate number; an optional business name and phone number may be shown (maximum 0.61 m²)
  • Whole-vehicle livery advertising is allowed for a single product or service with prior approval from the council

Meter and tariff

  • A taximeter is required, set to the current approved tariff, and must be visible to the passenger
  • A tariff card must be displayed in the vehicle

Temporary replacement vehicles

A temporary replacement licence ('T' plate) may be granted while your vehicle is off the road. Requirements:

  • Similar vehicle type
  • Maximum 10 years old
  • White in colour
  • Cannot be dual-licensed
  • Maximum licence period of 3 months

Apply with V5 logbook, insurance, MOT, inspection certificate, meter certificate and the applicable fee.

Private hire vehicle requirements

ℹ️
Private hire vehicles must not be white. Private hire vehicles must not display a roof sign.

Colour and signage

  • The vehicle colour must not be white
  • Roof signs are not permitted
  • Approved yellow door panels must show the company name, the words 'Private Hire' and 'Pre-Bookings Only'
  • The words 'taxi' or 'cab' must not appear anywhere on the vehicle or in advertising

Age and emissions

First licence
The vehicle must be no more than 7 years old and meet Euro 6. WAVs are exempt from the 7-year rule but must meet Euro 5 or above.
Maximum age
A vehicle cannot be licensed after its 15th anniversary of first registration.

Testing

  • Annual test at a council-approved testing station
  • Six-monthly tests once a vehicle is 8 years old

Required equipment

Temporary replacement vehicles

A temporary replacement licence may be granted. Requirements:

  • Similar vehicle type
  • Maximum 10 years old
  • Any colour except white
  • Cannot be dual-licensed
  • Maximum licence period of 3 months

Vehicle proprietor criminality check

An annual basic DBS check is required for vehicle proprietors, unless they are also a licensed driver with Gloucester City Council.
