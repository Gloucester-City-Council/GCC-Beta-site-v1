Vehicle licensing

Get a licence for your hackney carriage or private hire vehicle. Find out about vehicle standards, testing, plates, signage and fees.

Vehicle standards

Standards differ for hackney carriages and private hire vehicles. Select the type that applies to you.

Colour and wheelchair access

  • All hackney carriage vehicles must be white
  • All newly licensed hackney carriages must be wheelchair accessible

Age and emissions

  • First licence: the vehicle must be no more than 7 years old and meet Euro 6 standards
  • Wheelchair accessible vehicles (WAVs) are exempt from the 7-year first licence limit but must meet Euro 5 or higher
  • Vehicles cannot be relicensed after the 15th anniversary of first registration

Vehicle testing

  • Annual licensing authority test
  • Every 6 months from 8 years old
  • A valid MOT is required unless the vehicle is under one year old

Equipment requirements

  • Seat height: 30 inches; knee space: 10 inches; width per passenger: 16 inches; depth: 18 inches
  • BSEN3 1kg powder fire extinguisher (in date)
  • First aid kit meeting HSE minimum contents
  • Card payment machine (mandatory); receipt must show time, date, driver number and fare
  • CCTV or dashcam permitted with Licensing team approval; must comply with data protection law

Plates and signage

  • Rear exterior plate and interior plate – both must be clearly visible
  • Standardised illuminated 'TAXI' roof sign; illuminated when available for hire
  • Council recognition door panel including the city crest and plate number; optional business name and phone number (maximum 0.61m²)
  • Whole-vehicle advertising livery allowed for a single product or service with prior written approval

Taximeter

  • Meter must be inspected and set to the current approved tariff
  • Meter must be clearly visible to the passenger
  • A tariff card must be displayed in the vehicle

Temporary replacement vehicle

If your vehicle is off the road, you can apply for a temporary replacement. The replacement must be:

  • A similar type to your licensed vehicle
  • No more than 10 years old
  • White in colour
  • Not licensed by another authority at the same time

Temporary licences last up to 3 months. Apply with your V5, insurance, MOT, inspection certificate and meter certificate, plus the applicable fee. A 'T' plate will be issued.

Colour and signage

  • Private hire vehicles must not be white
  • Roof signs are not permitted
  • Approved yellow door panels must state the company name, 'Private Hire' and 'Pre-Bookings Only'
  • The words 'taxi' or 'cab' must not appear anywhere on the vehicle

Age and emissions

  • First licence: the vehicle must be no more than 7 years old and meet Euro 6 standards
  • Wheelchair accessible vehicles (WAVs) are exempt from the 7-year first licence limit but must meet Euro 5 or higher
  • Vehicles cannot be relicensed after the 15th anniversary of first registration

Vehicle testing

  • Annual licensing authority test
  • Every 6 months from 8 years old

Equipment requirements

  • Seat height: 30 inches; knee space: 10 inches; width per passenger: 16 inches; depth: 18 inches
  • BSEN3 1kg powder fire extinguisher (in date)
  • First aid kit meeting HSE minimum contents
  • Card payment machine (mandatory); provide a receipt on request
  • CCTV or dashcam permitted with approval; must comply with data protection law

Taximeter (if fitted)

  • Must be visible to the passenger
  • Must be inspected and set to the current approved tariff

Temporary replacement vehicle

If your vehicle is off the road, you can apply for a temporary replacement. The replacement must be:

  • A similar type to your licensed vehicle
  • No more than 10 years old
  • Any colour except white
  • Not licensed by another authority at the same time

Temporary licences last up to 3 months.

Vehicle proprietor check

If you own a licensed vehicle but are not also a licensed driver with Gloucester City Council, you must have an annual Basic DBS check.
