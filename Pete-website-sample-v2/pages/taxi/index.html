Hackney carriage and private hire licensing

Get a licence to drive, operate or own a hackney carriage or private hire vehicle in Gloucester. Find out what you need and how to apply.

Gloucester City Council licenses all hackney carriages (taxis) and private hire vehicles operating in the city. You must have the correct licence before you work or operate.

Before you apply

What you will need

  • Valid identification documents
  • Proof of address
  • Relevant certificates and qualifications
  • Disclosure and Barring Service (DBS) check
  • Payment for the application fee

Most applications are processed within 28 days of receiving all required documents. Some may take longer if additional checks are needed.
