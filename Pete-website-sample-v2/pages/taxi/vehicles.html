Vehicle licensing

Apply for or renew a licence for your hackney carriage or private hire vehicle. Find out about vehicle standards, inspections and approved testing stations.

Vehicle proprietor check

If you own a licensed vehicle but do not hold a Gloucester City Council driver licence, you must complete an annual Basic Disclosure and Barring Service (DBS) check.

Vehicle standards

Requirements differ depending on whether your vehicle is licensed as a hackney carriage or a private hire vehicle.

Colour

All hackney carriage vehicles must be white.

Wheelchair access

All newly licensed hackney carriage vehicles must be wheelchair accessible (WAV).

Age and emissions

  • New vehicles must be no more than 7 years old and meet the Euro 6 emissions standard
  • WAVs are exempt from the 7-year limit but must meet Euro 5 or better
  • Vehicles cannot be re-licensed after the 15th anniversary of first registration

Testing

  • Annual licensing authority test required
  • Six-monthly test for vehicles aged 8 years or more
  • Current MOT required (unless the vehicle is under one year old)

Plates and signage

  • Rear exterior plate and interior plate, both clearly visible
  • Standardised illuminated 'TAXI' roof sign, lit when available for hire
  • Council recognition door panels including city crest and plate number
  • Optional business name and phone number on door panels (maximum 0.61 m²)
  • Whole-vehicle advertising livery allowed for a single product or service with prior approval

Taximeter

A taximeter must be fitted and set to the current approved tariff. It must be visible to the passenger. A tariff card must be displayed.

Temporary replacement vehicle

If your vehicle is off the road, you can apply for a temporary replacement licence:

  • Replacement vehicle must be a similar type and white
  • Maximum age: 10 years
  • Maximum licence duration: 3 months
  • Dual licensing with another authority is not permitted
  • Apply with V5, insurance, MOT, inspection certificate and meter certificate, plus fee; a 'T' plate will be issued

Colour

Private hire vehicles must not be white.

Roof signs

Roof signs are not permitted on private hire vehicles.

Signage

Approved yellow door panels must show the company name, 'Private Hire' and 'Pre-Bookings Only'. The words 'taxi' or 'cab' must not appear.

Age and emissions

  • New vehicles must be no more than 7 years old and meet Euro 6 emissions standard
  • WAVs are exempt from the 7-year limit but must meet Euro 5 or better
  • Vehicles cannot be re-licensed after the 15th anniversary of first registration
  • Six-monthly test for vehicles aged 8 years or more

Equipment

Fire extinguisher
BSEN3 1kg dry powder, in date
First aid kit
HSE minimum contents
Card payment
Mandatory; receipt on request
CCTV or dashcam
Permitted with approval; must comply with data protection law

Seat dimensions

Minimum seat dimensions per passenger: height 30 in, knee space 10 in, width per passenger 16 in, depth 18 in.

Taximeter (if fitted)

If your vehicle has a taximeter it must be visible, inspected and set to the approved tariff.

Temporary replacement vehicle

  • Replacement vehicle must be a similar type and must not be white
  • Maximum age: 10 years
  • Maximum licence duration: 3 months
  • Dual licensing with another authority is not permitted
