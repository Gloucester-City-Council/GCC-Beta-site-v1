Newstershire Council Your new unitary council

Newstershire Council is a new unitary authority created from the reorganisation of local government in Gloucestershire. It brings together services previously delivered by the county council and the district and borough councils into one organisation.

2 Is it a county-level or district-level service?

Not sure which one?

County-level services (previously Gloucestershire County Council) include:

  • Schools, education and libraries
  • Adult social care
  • Children's services
  • Roads, highways and transport
  • Waste disposal (tips/recycling centres)
  • Fire & rescue

District-level services (previously your district or borough council) include:

  • Bins and waste collection
  • Council tax and housing benefit
  • Planning and building control
  • Housing
  • Leisure and parks
  • Environmental health
