Welcome to Gloucestershire Council

The new single council for the whole of Gloucestershire. Tell us where you live and we'll point you to the right website for the service you need.

650k Residents served
7 Councils merged into one
300+ Council services

Gloucestershire Council is a new unitary authority. We've replaced Gloucestershire County Council and the six district and borough councils with one council for the whole county. While we bring everything together, some services still live on the former councils' websites — the finder below points you to the right website.
