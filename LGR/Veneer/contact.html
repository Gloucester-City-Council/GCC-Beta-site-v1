Looking for a specific service?

We're still bringing services together under the new council. For many things — bins, council tax, planning, housing and more — you'll currently be served by one of the former district or borough councils, or by county-level teams.

Accessibility and other formats

We want everyone to be able to reach us. We can provide information in large print, easy read, braille or audio, and arrange interpretation, including British Sign Language (BSL), on request.

  • Text relay: dial 18001 then our number
  • Ask about a face-to-face or video BSL interpreter when you contact us
  • Tell us if you need documents in a different format and we'll arrange it

Complaints, feedback and information requests
