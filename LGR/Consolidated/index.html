Welcome to Newstershire Council

We're the new unitary authority for Gloucestershire, bringing county and district services together under one roof. Use the tool below to find the right service for where you live.

650k Residents served
6 Districts & boroughs
300+ Council services

Newstershire Council is a new unitary authority formed from the reorganisation of local government in Gloucestershire. It brings together services previously delivered by the county council and the district and borough councils.

County-level services (previously Gloucestershire County Council) include:

  • Schools, education and libraries
  • Adult social care
  • Children's services
  • Roads, highways and transport
  • Waste disposal (tips / recycling centres)
  • Fire & rescue

District-level services (previously your district or borough council) include:

  • Bins and waste collection
  • Council tax and housing benefit
  • Planning and building control
  • Housing
  • Leisure and parks
  • Environmental health
