Welcome to Newstershire Council

Newstershire Council is a new unitary authority. We've replaced Gloucestershire County Council and the six district and borough councils with one council for the whole county. While we bring everything together, some services still live on the former councils' websites.

Some services are still moving across from the former councils. Answer two quick questions and we'll point you to the right existing council website.

2 Is it a county or district service?

Not sure which one?

County-level services (previously Gloucestershire County Council) include:

  • Schools, education and libraries
  • Adult social care
  • Children's services
  • Roads, highways and transport
  • Waste disposal (tips / recycling centres)
  • Fire & rescue

District-level services (previously your district or borough council) include:

  • Bins and waste collection
  • Council tax and housing benefit
  • Planning and building control
  • Housing
  • Leisure and parks
  • Environmental health
