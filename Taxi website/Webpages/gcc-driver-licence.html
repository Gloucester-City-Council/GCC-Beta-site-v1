Driver licence

Apply for a new licence or renew an existing one. This page covers both hackney carriage and private hire driver licences.

What do you need?
Type of licence

Check you are eligible

  • ✓ Hold a full UK driving licence (not provisional) for at least 12 continuous months
  • ✓ Be aged 21 or over
  • ✓ Have no more than 3 penalty points on your DVLA licence
  • ✓
    Be a fit and proper person

    A licence will not be granted unless the Licensing Authority is satisfied that you are a fit and proper person.

    You must declare any convictions, cautions, fixed penalties or pending court cases that arise between submitting your application and your licence being issued.

  • ✓
    Be eligible to work in the UK

    A right to work check will be carried out automatically before your licence is issued.

    Nationality and visa requirements

    Student visas

    Applications will not be accepted from people who hold a student visa.

    Student visas

    Foreign nationals that hold a Student Visa are only permitted to work for up to 20 hours during term time (depending on the course studied). The private hire operator may be liable if the driver breaches the limitations of the student visa. Information may be shared with the Border Agency.

    Lived outside the UK

    If you have lived outside the UK since the age of 18, you must provide a Certificate of Good Conduct, a Criminal Record Check or similar document from each country where you have been resident for more than 6 months in a calendar year. If the certificate is not in English, provide a translation from the Embassy or Consulate of that country.

    Non-EU or EEA driving licence

    If your driving licence was issued outside the EU or EEA, you must convert it to a GB DVLA licence before applying. The convertible licence can be used to demonstrate 12 months of driving experience.

Gather your documents and apply

Have everything below ready before you start. You will not be able to save and return to your application.

  • Operator confirmation

    You must have confirmation from the private hire operator you will be working for.

  • Photograph

    Must meet the gov.uk passport photo requirements.

  • DVLA driving licence

    Your current valid DVLA photocard licence — both the front and back. If your licence was issued before 1998, provide the paper licence showing your current home address.

  • DVLA check code

    Generate a check code at gov.uk/view-driving-licence. Codes are valid for 21 days, can only be used once, and are case sensitive.

  • DBS certificate

    An enhanced DBS certificate registered with the DBS Online Update Service. Apply via TaxiPlus.

  • Pass certificates from all tests and assessments

    Knowledge test, safeguarding, English proficiency and driving assessment. Book online.

  • Driving medical certificate

    Your GP must complete this to the standard required for professional drivers. No more than 3 months old, on the approved medical form.

  • Application fee

    Paid when you submit your application online.

    1 year — £132.00 3 years — £289.00

Renew before your licence expires

A licence renewed after the expiry date is not valid — you cannot drive until a new licence is issued. There is no period of grace and you will need to make a full new application, including a new criminal record check and medical report.

  • ✓ You must remain eligible to work in the UK. If your circumstances have changed since your last licence was issued you may need to provide evidence.

Gather your documents and renew

  • Operator confirmation

    You must have confirmation from the private hire operator you will be working for.

  • Photograph

    Must meet the gov.uk passport photo requirements.

  • DVLA driving licence

    Your current valid DVLA photocard licence — both the front and back. If your licence was issued before 1998, provide the paper licence showing your current home address.

  • DVLA check code

    Generate a check code at gov.uk/view-driving-licence. Codes are valid for 21 days, can only be used once, and are case sensitive.

  • DBS certificate

    Your DBS subscription must remain active. We conduct 6-monthly checks automatically. If your subscription has lapsed you will need a new certificate. Apply via TaxiPlus.

  • Driving medical certificate

    Your GP must complete this to the standard required for professional drivers. No more than 3 months old, on the approved medical form.

    If you are aged 45 or over: required every 5 years.

    If you are aged 65 or over: required every year.

  • Safeguarding refresher certificate

    Required every 3 years. Your licence will be suspended if training is overdue. Book online.

  • Renewal fee

    Paid when you submit your renewal online.

    1 year — £93.00 3 years — £241.00
