Something has changed

Notify us of a change to your licence, vehicle or personal circumstances.

Driver and vehicle licence changes

  • Change of address

    You must notify us of any change to your home address. Applies to both driver and vehicle licences. A fee applies.

    Apply online →
  • Replacement badge or plate

    Apply for a replacement driver badge or vehicle licence plate. Applies to both driver and vehicle licences. A fee applies.

    Apply online →
  • Notify us of a conviction, caution or fixed penalty

    You must notify us within 48 hours of any conviction, caution, fixed penalty or pending court case.

    Download form →

Vehicle licence changes

  • Transfer of ownership

    If you are taking over a licensed vehicle from another proprietor. A fee applies.

    Apply online →
  • Change of vehicle

    If you want to swap your licensed vehicle for a different one. New plates will be issued. A fee applies.

    Apply online →
  • Change of vehicle registration

    If the registration number of your licensed vehicle has changed. You must provide confirmation from DVLA.

    Apply online →
  • Private hire vehicle plate exemption

    Apply to exempt your private hire vehicle from displaying licence plates and signage. Each application is assessed on its own merits. Fleet exemptions are not permitted.

    Apply online →

Report an accident

  • Report a hackney carriage or private hire accident

    If you are involved in an accident while driving a licensed vehicle you must report it to us.
