Your bins

Most homes in Gloucester have three bins:

Black bin (general waste)

Collected every two weeks. Use this for items that cannot be recycled.

Green bin (recycling)

Collected every two weeks. Use this for paper, cardboard, cans, glass and plastic bottles.

Food waste caddy

Collected every week. Use this for all food waste including meat, fish and dairy.

Collection times

Put your bins out by 7am on collection day. Collections take place between 7am and 4pm.

Please bring your bins back in by the end of collection day.

Recycling centres

You can take items to the household recycling centre at:

Hempsted Recycling Centre
Hempsted Lane
Gloucester
GL2 5LA

Open 7 days a week, 9am to 5pm.

You do not need to book. You can bring items like:

  • furniture
  • electrical items
  • garden waste
  • DIY waste (limited amounts)
  • textiles and clothing
