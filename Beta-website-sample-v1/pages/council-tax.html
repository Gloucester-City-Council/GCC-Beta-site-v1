About council tax

Council tax is a charge on homes that helps pay for local services. The amount you pay depends on the value of your home and who lives there.

Your council tax bill covers services provided by:

  • Gloucester City Council
  • Gloucestershire County Council
  • Gloucestershire Police
  • Gloucestershire Fire and Rescue

When to pay

Council tax is usually paid over 10 months from April to January. You can ask to spread payments over 12 months.

We will send you a bill in March each year. The bill shows how much you need to pay and when.

If you cannot pay

Contact us as soon as possible if you are struggling to pay. We may be able to help with:

  • a different payment plan
  • council tax support to reduce your bill
  • advice about other benefits you could claim
