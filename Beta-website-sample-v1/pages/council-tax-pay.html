Pay council tax

You can pay your council tax online, by direct debit, or by other methods. The quickest way to pay is online or by direct debit.

Before you start

To pay online you will need:

  • your council tax account number (this is on your bill)
  • a debit or credit card

Paying takes about 5 minutes.

You can pay all or part of your bill. You do not need to sign in.

Start now

Set up a direct debit

A direct debit is the easiest way to pay. Your payments come out of your bank account each month.

To set up a direct debit you will need:

  • your council tax account number
  • your bank account number
  • your bank sort code

You can choose to pay on the 1st, 8th, 15th or 22nd of each month.

Set up a direct debit online

Other ways to pay

By phone

Call our payment line on 01onal11 232 6700. You will need your account number and a debit or credit card.

At a bank or Post Office

You can pay at any bank or Post Office using the barcode on your bill. You will need to take your bill with you.

By bank transfer

You can pay by bank transfer using these details:

  • Account name: Gloucester City Council
  • Sort code: 00-00-00
  • Account number: 00000000
  • Reference: use your council tax account number

Important

Always use your council tax account number as the payment reference. This makes sure your payment goes to the right account.

If you are struggling to pay

Contact us as soon as possible if you cannot pay your bill. We can discuss:

  • setting up a payment plan
  • council tax support to reduce your bill
  • other help that may be available

The sooner you contact us, the more options we have to help you.

Phone: 01onal11 232 6700

Email: counciltax@gloucester.gov.uk
